About the schemes
Custom URL schemes are claimed by an installed app's manifest (iOS LSApplicationQueriesSchemes / Info.plist; Android intent filters). Schemes shown here are a mix of community-observed strings and educated guesses. Add or remove rows by editing SCHEMES in index.html.
